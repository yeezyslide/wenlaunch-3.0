Questions

FAQ

How much does a project cost?

Marketing websites start at $8,000, e-commerce stores start at $6,000, and landing pages start at $3,000. Final pricing depends on scope, number of pages, and complexity. We'll cover everything on the discovery call.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects are delivered in 4-6 weeks. Landing pages can be faster (2-3 weeks). We'll set a clear timeline during kickoff.

What's included in the price?

Everything from research and wireframes to visual design, Framer development, and launch. You get a fully functional, SEO-ready website that your team can update without a developer.

Why Framer?

Framer gives you the best of both worlds — design-quality results with the flexibility of a CMS your team can manage. It's faster to build, faster to load, and doesn't require ongoing developer maintenance like WordPress. We've built 100+ sites in Framer and it's the only platform we work in.

Is Mason directly involved in the work?

Yes. Mason is the Creative Director and is personally involved in every project — from strategy and design direction to final QA before launch. Our in-house design team works alongside Mason to deliver faster without sacrificing quality.

What happens after launch?

We include 30 days of post-launch support for any tweaks or issues. We also offer ongoing maintenance if needed. And since it's built in Framer, your team can make content updates themselves.
